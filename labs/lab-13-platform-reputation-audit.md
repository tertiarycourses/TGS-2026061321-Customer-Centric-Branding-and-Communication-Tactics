# Activity 13 — Platform Reputation Audit

**Learning Unit:** LU4 — Branding Effectiveness  |  **Activity type:** In-class workshop  |  **Course:** Customer-Centric Branding and Communication Tactics (TGS-2026061321)

## Objective

Identify measures or indicators of the organisation's reputation on different platforms.

## Scenario

Your organisation needs to establish reputation monitoring for a new product launch across social media, review sites, and business platforms.

## What you will produce

A reputation-monitoring framework with defined metrics and tracking systems.

**Duration:** 20 minutes

## Steps

### Step 1

Map the stakeholder groups and select 3 key platforms per group.

### Step 2

Define specific KPIs for the social, digital and business indicators.

### Step 3

Set up monitoring tools and establish baseline measurements.

### Step 4

Create a weekly reporting dashboard showing sentiment trends.

## Check your work (Debrief it)

Present the comprehensive reputation-monitoring framework with its metrics and tracking system.

## Deliverable

Save your output — it forms part of your LU4 workbook, which you may draw on for the open-book Case Study assessment on Day 2.
