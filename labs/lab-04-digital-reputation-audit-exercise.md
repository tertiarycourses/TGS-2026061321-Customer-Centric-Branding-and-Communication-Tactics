# Activity 4 — Digital Reputation Audit Exercise

**Learning Unit:** LU1 — Stakeholders and Organisation  |  **Activity type:** In-class workshop  |  **Course:** Customer-Centric Branding and Communication Tactics (TGS-2026061321)

## Objective

Assess the organisation's reputation on social media and other platforms.

## Scenario

Your organisation has received mixed customer feedback across social platforms. Conduct a reputation assessment to identify improvement opportunities and develop an action plan.

## What you will produce

A reputation-assessment report covering 3 platforms with sentiment breakdown and an action plan.

**Duration:** 45 minutes

## Steps

### Step 1

Select three platforms (e.g. Google, Facebook, one competitor platform).

### Step 2

Document 10 recent mentions/reviews per platform.

### Step 3

Categorise each piece of feedback as positive, neutral or negative.

### Step 4

Identify three recurring themes or issues across the feedback.

### Step 5

Develop a targeted response strategy for each theme.

## Check your work (Debrief it)

Present the reputation-assessment report — platform-by-platform analysis, an overall sentiment breakdown, the three key themes, and a strategic recommendation for each.

## Deliverable

Save your output — it forms part of your LU1 workbook, which you may draw on for the open-book Case Study assessment on Day 2.
