# Activity 17 — PR Campaign Audit Exercise

**Learning Unit:** LU4 — Branding Effectiveness  |  **Activity type:** In-class workshop  |  **Course:** Customer-Centric Branding and Communication Tactics (TGS-2026061321)

## Objective

Provide suggestions to improve public-relations campaign effectiveness.

## Scenario

Your organisation's recent product-launch campaign achieved 500K impressions but only 15% top-tier coverage and 45% message penetration. Stakeholders are questioning the campaign's business impact.

## What you will produce

An improvement proposal with metrics, built using the AMEC measurement framework.

**Duration:** 25 minutes

## Steps

### Step 1

Apply the AMEC framework to identify the performance gaps.

### Step 2

Develop three strategic recommendations using best practice.

### Step 3

Create a measurement plan with specific KPIs.

## Check your work (Debrief it)

Present the comprehensive improvement proposal — the three recommendations must each map to a specific gap surfaced by the AMEC analysis.

## Deliverable

Save your output — it forms part of your LU4 workbook, which you may draw on for the open-book Case Study assessment on Day 2.
