# Activity 7 — Campaign Documentation Audit

**Learning Unit:** LU2 — Customer Influence  |  **Activity type:** In-class workshop  |  **Course:** Customer-Centric Branding and Communication Tactics (TGS-2026061321)

## Objective

Document customer reception to the brand and the outcome of branding campaigns.

## Scenario

Your company launched a new product branding campaign three months ago. Management needs a documentation report on customer reception and campaign outcomes to inform future marketing investment.

## What you will produce

A structured campaign-reception report with metrics, analysis and optimisation recommendations.

**Duration:** 25 minutes

## Steps

### Step 1

Identify three key metrics from the campaign data.

### Step 2

Analyse customer feedback patterns and sentiment trends.

### Step 3

Create a documentation framework for ongoing measurement.

### Step 4

Present the findings with actionable recommendations.

## Check your work (Debrief it)

Present the structured campaign-reception report, including the documentation framework that will be reused for the next campaign.

## Deliverable

Save your output — it forms part of your LU2 workbook, which you may draw on for the open-book Case Study assessment on Day 2.
