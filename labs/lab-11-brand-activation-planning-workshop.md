# Activity 11 — Brand Activation Planning Workshop

**Learning Unit:** LU3 — Branding in Marketing  |  **Activity type:** In-class workshop  |  **Course:** Customer-Centric Branding and Communication Tactics (TGS-2026061321)

## Objective

Execute branding campaigns, events and activities to increase brand awareness.

## Scenario

Your organisation wants to launch a new product line and needs to increase brand awareness by 40% within 6 months. Design a multi-channel campaign that includes both digital and experiential elements.

## What you will produce

A campaign-strategy document with timeline, budget allocation and success metrics.

**Duration:** 30 minutes

## Steps

### Step 1

Define the target audience's demographics and psychographics.

### Step 2

Develop a core brand message aligned with the audience's values.

### Step 3

Select 3–4 marketing channels and design consistent visuals across them.

### Step 4

Plan one experiential event and its supporting digital content.

### Step 5

Create a measurement framework using brand-recall metrics.

## Check your work (Debrief it)

Present the complete campaign-strategy document with timeline, budget allocation and success metrics — the trainer checks the digital and experiential elements are both present and consistent.

## Deliverable

Save your output — it forms part of your LU3 workbook, which you may draw on for the open-book Case Study assessment on Day 2.
