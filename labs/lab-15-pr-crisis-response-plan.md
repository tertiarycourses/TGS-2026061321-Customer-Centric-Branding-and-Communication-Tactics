# Activity 15 — PR Crisis Response Plan

**Learning Unit:** LU4 — Branding Effectiveness  |  **Activity type:** In-class workshop  |  **Course:** Customer-Centric Branding and Communication Tactics (TGS-2026061321)

## Objective

Apply public-relations tactics.

## Scenario

Your company faces negative social-media coverage about product-quality issues. Design a comprehensive PR response strategy using the tactics learned.

## What you will produce

A crisis-communication plan with timelines and responsible parties.

**Duration:** 25 minutes

## Steps

### Step 1

Identify the affected stakeholder groups and media outlets.

### Step 2

Develop core messaging that addresses the concerns transparently.

### Step 3

Create a media-outreach plan with personalised pitches.

### Step 4

Design the social-media response and community-engagement plan.

### Step 5

Establish measurement metrics for reputation recovery.

## Check your work (Debrief it)

Present the complete crisis-communication plan with a timeline and the party responsible for each action.

## Deliverable

Save your output — it forms part of your LU4 workbook, which you may draw on for the open-book Case Study assessment on Day 2.
