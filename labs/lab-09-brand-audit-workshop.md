# Activity 9 — Brand Audit Workshop

**Learning Unit:** LU3 — Branding in Marketing  |  **Activity type:** In-class workshop  |  **Course:** Customer-Centric Branding and Communication Tactics (TGS-2026061321)

## Objective

Explain the basics of branding.

## Scenario

You are consulting for a local restaurant with inconsistent branding across their website, social media, and physical location. The owner wants to understand how to create unified brand guidelines.

## What you will produce

A brand-guidelines document — visual standards, voice guidelines and an implementation roadmap.

**Duration:** 30 minutes

## Steps

### Step 1

Analyse the current brand touchpoints and identify inconsistencies.

### Step 2

Define the core brand values and a mission statement.

### Step 3

Create visual identity specifications — logo and colours.

### Step 4

Develop brand-voice guidelines for customer communication.

### Step 5

Design an implementation plan with specific application examples.

## Check your work (Debrief it)

Present the comprehensive brand-guidelines document — visual standards, voice guidelines, and the implementation roadmap.

## Deliverable

Save your output — it forms part of your LU3 workbook, which you may draw on for the open-book Case Study assessment on Day 2.
