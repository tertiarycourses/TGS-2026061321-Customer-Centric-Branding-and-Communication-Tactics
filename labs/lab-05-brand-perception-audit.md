# Activity 5 — Brand Perception Audit

**Learning Unit:** LU2 — Customer Influence  |  **Activity type:** In-class workshop  |  **Course:** Customer-Centric Branding and Communication Tactics (TGS-2026061321)

## Objective

Assess customer perceptions of the brand, products and services.

## Scenario

Your company recently received mixed customer reviews. Management believes service quality is excellent, but customer-satisfaction scores are declining. Conduct a perception audit to identify the gaps.

## What you will produce

A perception-gap analysis report comparing internal quality metrics with customer feedback.

**Duration:** 25 minutes

## Steps

### Step 1

Survey 20 recent customers about their service experience.

### Step 2

Compare internal quality metrics with the customer feedback.

### Step 3

Identify the top 3 perception gaps.

### Step 4

Develop an action plan to address the gaps.

## Check your work (Debrief it)

Present the perception-gap analysis report with improvement recommendations for each of the top 3 gaps.

## Deliverable

Save your output — it forms part of your LU2 workbook, which you may draw on for the open-book Case Study assessment on Day 2.
