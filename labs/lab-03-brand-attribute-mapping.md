# Activity 3 — Brand Attribute Mapping

**Learning Unit:** LU1 — Stakeholders and Organisation  |  **Activity type:** In-class workshop  |  **Course:** Customer-Centric Branding and Communication Tactics (TGS-2026061321)

## Objective

Draft branding designs and ideas highlighting the product or service's attributes and benefits.

## Scenario

Design a brand identity for a new fitness app targeting busy professionals, mapping both hard attributes (features) and soft attributes (lifestyle benefits) into a compelling visual concept.

## What you will produce

A brand attribute map (3 hard + 3 soft attributes), a mood board and a logo concept.

**Duration:** 30 minutes

## Steps

### Step 1

List 3 hard attributes and 3 soft attributes for the fitness app.

### Step 2

Create a visual mood board that represents the brand personality.

### Step 3

Design a logo concept that incorporates the key attributes.

### Step 4

Present a brand narrative that connects the features to the benefits.

## Check your work (Debrief it)

Present the complete brand attribute map alongside the visual concept, showing a clear line from each hard attribute to the soft benefit it supports.

## Deliverable

Save your output — it forms part of your LU1 workbook, which you may draw on for the open-book Case Study assessment on Day 2.
