# Activity 16 — KPI Dashboard Design

**Learning Unit:** LU4 — Branding Effectiveness  |  **Activity type:** In-class workshop  |  **Course:** Customer-Centric Branding and Communication Tactics (TGS-2026061321)

## Objective

Monitor the success of the brand against Key Performance Indicators (KPI).

## Scenario

Create a brand KPI monitoring framework for a retail company launching new product lines.

## What you will produce

A complete KPI framework with a tracking schedule.

**Duration:** 20 minutes

## Steps

### Step 1

Select 3 internal and 3 external KPIs.

### Step 2

Define the measure, target, data source and frequency for each KPI.

### Step 3

Design a monthly tracking dashboard.

## Check your work (Debrief it)

Present the complete KPI framework with its tracking schedule — 3 internal + 3 external KPIs, each with a measure, target, source and frequency.

## Deliverable

Save your output — it forms part of your LU4 workbook, which you may draw on for the open-book Case Study assessment on Day 2.
