# Activity 14 — Brand Health Assessment

**Learning Unit:** LU4 — Branding Effectiveness  |  **Activity type:** In-class workshop  |  **Course:** Customer-Centric Branding and Communication Tactics (TGS-2026061321)

## Objective

Recognise the indicators of successful branding.

## Scenario

You are a marketing manager evaluating your company's brand performance using both traditional and digital metrics.

## What you will produce

A brand-health scorecard with metric definitions, current performance and an improvement plan.

**Duration:** 25 minutes

## Steps

### Step 1

Select 3 key metrics from the awareness, loyalty and performance categories.

### Step 2

Design a measurement approach that combines survey and digital analytics.

### Step 3

Create a monthly tracking dashboard with benchmarks.

### Step 4

Present the findings with actionable recommendations.

## Check your work (Debrief it)

Present the brand-health scorecard — metric definitions, current performance and the improvement plan.

## Deliverable

Save your output — it forms part of your LU4 workbook, which you may draw on for the open-book Case Study assessment on Day 2.
