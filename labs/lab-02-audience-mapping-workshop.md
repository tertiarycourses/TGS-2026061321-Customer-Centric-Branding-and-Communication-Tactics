# Activity 2 — Audience Mapping Workshop

**Learning Unit:** LU1 — Stakeholders and Organisation  |  **Activity type:** In-class workshop  |  **Course:** Customer-Centric Branding and Communication Tactics (TGS-2026061321)

## Objective

Recognise the types of external audience and their needs.

## Scenario

Your company is launching a new sustainability product line. Map external audiences and develop targeted messaging strategies for each segment.

## What you will produce

An audience mapping matrix covering 4 external segments with tailored messaging and channels.

**Duration:** 25 minutes

## Steps

### Step 1

Identify four primary external audience segments for the product.

### Step 2

Research the demographic and psychographic profile of each segment.

### Step 3

Create a tailored key message for each segment, emphasising the relevant benefit.

### Step 4

Select the appropriate communication channel(s) for each audience.

### Step 5

Present the audience mapping matrix with its messaging strategy.

## Check your work (Debrief it)

Present a comprehensive audience-analysis matrix with segment profiles and a customised communication plan per segment.

## Deliverable

Save your output — it forms part of your LU1 workbook, which you may draw on for the open-book Case Study assessment on Day 2.
