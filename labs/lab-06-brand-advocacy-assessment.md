# Activity 6 — Brand Advocacy Assessment

**Learning Unit:** LU2 — Customer Influence  |  **Activity type:** In-class workshop  |  **Course:** Customer-Centric Branding and Communication Tactics (TGS-2026061321)

## Objective

Recognise the importance of the customer in influencing brand reputation.

## Scenario

Your company recently received mixed reviews online. As brand manager, analyse customer feedback and develop an advocacy strategy to improve brand reputation through existing satisfied customers.

## What you will produce

A customer-advocacy strategy document with target impact metrics.

**Duration:** 25 minutes

## Steps

### Step 1

Review the provided customer feedback data.

### Step 2

Identify satisfied customers for the advocacy programme.

### Step 3

Design an advocacy campaign that leverages their positive experiences.

### Step 4

Present the strategy together with its expected impact metrics.

## Check your work (Debrief it)

Present a comprehensive advocacy strategy document with named target metrics and how they will be tracked.

## Deliverable

Save your output — it forms part of your LU2 workbook, which you may draw on for the open-book Case Study assessment on Day 2.
