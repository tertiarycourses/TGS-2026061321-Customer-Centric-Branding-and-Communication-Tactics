# Activity 1 — Stakeholder Influence Mapping

**Learning Unit:** LU1 — Stakeholders and Organisation  |  **Activity type:** In-class workshop  |  **Course:** Customer-Centric Branding and Communication Tactics (TGS-2026061321)

## Objective

Identify internal and external stakeholders influencing the brand.

## Scenario

Your company is launching a new sustainability initiative. Map internal and external stakeholders, assess their influence levels, and design engagement strategies for each group.

## What you will produce

A stakeholder influence matrix (12 stakeholders) with an engagement strategy for each group.

**Duration:** 25 minutes

## Steps

### Step 1

List 6 internal and 6 external stakeholders for the sustainability initiative.

### Step 2

Rate each stakeholder's influence on a 1–5 scale.

### Step 3

Identify each stakeholder's primary brand concern.

### Step 4

Design a specific communication approach for each group.

## Check your work (Debrief it)

Present a completed stakeholder influence matrix with an engagement strategy for every stakeholder — the trainer checks that internal and external groups are both represented and that each strategy matches the stakeholder's stated concern.

## Deliverable

Save your output — it forms part of your LU1 workbook, which you may draw on for the open-book Case Study assessment on Day 2.
